Science

The institution offers a diverse range of science courses, including Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.), Master of Philosophy (M.Phil.), Master of Science (M.Sc), Master of Computer Applications (M.C.A), and specialized programs such as M.Sc Medical Biochemistry, M.Sc bio-technology, and M.Tech. (Atmospheric Physics). These programs cover various disciplines such as Computer Science, Electronics, Bio-Informatics, Communication Studies, Material Science, and more, with specific entrance qualifications and durations tailored to each course.

Commerce

The Faculty of Commerce offers a diverse array of courses, including doctoral and master's programs such as Ph.D. and M.Phil., as well as undergraduate degrees like B.Com., along with specialized postgraduate diplomas in areas such as company secretaryship, salesmanship, advertisement, statistics, taxation, banking, accountancy, and international business. Additionally, a certificate course in E-commerce is available for students completing their T.Y.B.Com.

Management

The Faculty of Management offers diverse programs, including Ph.D., M.B.A., M.P.M., M.C.M., M.M.S., and M.M.M. Admission to M.B.A. requires a graduate degree with 50% marks (45% for reserved categories) and a selection process. Undergraduate options include B.H.M.C.T., and diploma courses cover Business Management, Computer Management, Marketing Management, Print Administration, Co-operative Management, Hospital Management, Office Management, Capital Marketing Management, Financial Services, and Environment. Part-time offerings like Advanced Diploma in Computer Application (A.D.C.A.) cater to graduates meeting specific criteria.

Our Facilities

At our university, top-notch facilities provide a foundation for success, offering cutting-edge resources and spaces designed to nurture talent, research, and a vibrant campus life.

Jayakar knowledge Resource Centre

Jayakar Knowledge Resource Centre (formerly Jayakar Library) is central library of Savitribai Phule Pune University. It was established in January 1950, and is named in honor of the first Vice-Chancellor of the university Dr. M. R. Jayakar Participates in E-ShodhSindhu program. Under this program access to more than 8000 journals are provided. It is a part of Universal Digital Library program since 2002. Implementated RFID technology in the library along with smart cards to faculty and students. Has internet center for students of the University.

Sports & Physical education

Dr. Deepak Mane, Director, Board of Sports & Physical Education
The University Board of Sports & Physical Education came into existence in the year 1949. The objective of the Board of Sports & physical Education is to take necessary steps for promotion of sports culture and activities in the field of sports in colleges and university departments and also to establish links with regional and national bodies in the various sports and to promote various activities jointly with them in colleges, recognized institutions and university departments as per the policy of the university.

Food Court

The university's food court is a bustling hub where students can indulge in a variety of culinary delights, offering a diverse menu to cater to different tastes. With its modern ambiance and comfortable seating, the food court serves as a social gathering space for students to relax and enjoy delicious meals between classes. From quick bites to wholesome meals, the food court provides a range of options, making it a convenient and essential part of campus life. It not only satisfies hunger but also fosters a sense of community as students come together to share meals and create lasting memories in this lively setting.
